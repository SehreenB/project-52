About Us

Our Website is Designed to provide you with easy to follow origami designs. This site is perfect for beginners or anyone who is interested in trying out the Japanese art of origami! Origami is fun, easy and inexpensive!

The word "origami" comes from 2 different words, "ori" meaning fold, and "kami" meaning paper. It is an activity that has been passed down for generations and generations so our mission is to ennsure that it continues to be passes down.

With today's technological advances, it is easier than ever to do so. Our collection of tutorials consists of diagrams that make it very easy to create all sorts of animals using just a square peice of paper!
